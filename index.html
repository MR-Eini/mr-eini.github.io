Research Interests

Developing innovative methodologies to assess the impacts of climate change on long-term surface and subsurface water storage, flood and drought occurrences, ecosystem health, and agricultural productivity. My research focuses on integrating novel modeling techniques with advanced observational data to create comprehensive models that can predict and mitigate adverse environmental effects. I aim to enhance the accuracy and efficiency of hydrological models by incorporating sustainable technologies and local practices. Additionally, I am interested in exploring the socio-economic implications of water resource management, working collaboratively with stakeholders to design and implement adaptive strategies that ensure water security and sustainability in the face of changing climatic conditions.
