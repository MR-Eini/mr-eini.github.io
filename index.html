About Me

I am passionate about developing innovative methodologies to assess the impacts of climate change on water resources. My research focuses on integrating novel modeling techniques with advanced observational data to create comprehensive models that can predict and mitigate adverse environmental effects.

Research Interests

My research focuses on:

  • Climate change impacts on water storage, flood, and drought occurrences
  • Enhancing the accuracy of hydrological models
  • Sustainable technologies in water resource management
