About Me

As a researcher in Hydrology, my work focuses on developing innovative methodologies to assess the impacts of climate change on water resources. I aim to integrate novel modeling techniques with advanced observational data to create comprehensive models that can predict and mitigate environmental effects.

ResearchGate Google Scholar LinkedIn Scopus

Research Interests

My research focuses on assessing the impacts of climate change on long-term water storage, floods, and droughts, as well as ecosystem health and agricultural productivity.
